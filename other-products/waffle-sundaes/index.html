Waffle sundaes

Waffle sundaes are two waffles, stacked on top of each other, with a big scoop of ice cream along with fresh blueberries, strawberries, raspberries, and blackberries.

The two waffles can be any color you want and have any fillings you want, but only one of the waffles can have toppings, which go on top of the ice cream and berries. In addition, the ice cream can be any flavor you want.

Waffle sundaes cost $1.49 and come with up to 3 fillings for each waffle and 2 additional toppings for the sundae. Each additional filling costs $0.03 per waffle and each additional topping costs $0.04.

Rito and waffles

Rito and waffles is just two regular moderator waffles, but you get some fried rito on top of each of them! (Rito meat is similar to chicken meat, but with a stronger taste)

Because of the rito meat, there are only two toppings you can get with rito and waffles: maple syrup and assorted berries, which are both free. Both will be available for regular waffles soon.

Rito and waffles costs $0.99, and you can get up to 4 fillings in each waffle; each additional filling is $0.03 per waffle.

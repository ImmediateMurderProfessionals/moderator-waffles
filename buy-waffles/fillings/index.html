Fillings

You can ask for many different fillings within the waffles you order.

Current list of fillings:

  • Chocolate chips- Just your average delicious chocolate chips.
  • Blueberries- All-natural blueberries with no pesticides or other yucky slop.
  • Cinnamon- Used in cuisine since ancient times. There's even a type of fox named after it!
  • M&Ms- The M&Ms you all know and love. Not to be confused with Moxxie and Millie.
  • Octopus- Octopus is one of my favorite meats, why shouldn't I put it in waffles?
  • NyQuil- Perfect if you're infected with the common cold. Goes well with chicken.
  • Stolas- The Bulgarian prince that was executed in November 2024, now in your waffles!
  • Delphox pills- Perfect if your Delphox has COVID-19. May cause strange side effects if consumed by a human.
  • Fuzzy ooze- Imported fresh from Alterna. We don't know what happens if you eat it. Can also be used as a topping.
  • Latios- A legendary filling for the one person who requested this.
  • Marbled rock roast- Popular volcanic cuisine. It's "hard" to not get addicted to it!
  • Strange matter- A hypothetical substance that could destroy the world. It's strangely tasty. Can also be used as a topping.
  • Pink sauce- Popular across several well-known CCP-owned social media sites. Probably doesn't cause botulism. Can also be used as a topping.
  • Lynel horns- Tougher than most other fillings. Has a weak meaty taste. One of my favorite ingredients ever since 2021.
  • Blood- Real human blood. Has been cleaned so you do not get AIDS or other nasty diseases. Can also be used as a topping.
  • Marijuana- Used for thousands of years. May be addictive in some. Only available where legal.
  • Stupid sauce- A popular sauce in strange 90s computer games. May cause hallucinaions. Can also be used as a topping.
  • Oil- A black liquid that makes cars go, among other things. Screws up the enviroment, unless it's in our delicious waffles. Can also be used as a topping.
  • Mystery meat- A mysterious meat that changes regularly. Even I don't know what it is.
